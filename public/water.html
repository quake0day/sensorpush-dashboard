How to Calibrate

1. Click Refresh to load a camera snapshot
2. Click New Level below, then use the Draw tool to trace the water edge along the rocks
3. Set a label (e.g. "Normal") and height in cm
4. Repeat for other water levels (wait for water to change, or use photos from different times)
5. Click Save to store calibration
